# Awesome Health Datasets

A curated list of open datasets in Healthcare, Medical Imaging, Clinical Data, and Bioinformatics. Updated for 2025/2026.

## 🏥 Clinical & Electronic Health Records (EHR)

- [MIMIC-IV](https://physionet.org/content/mimiciv/) - Comprehensive critical care dataset containing de-identified health records of ICU patients (2008–2019).
- [eICU Collaborative Research Database](https://eicu-crd.mit.edu/) - Multi-center critical care dataset of clinical data from over 200,000 ICU stays.
- [All of Us (NIH)](https://www.allofus.nih.gov/) - Diverse EHR & genomics dataset focusing on underrepresented populations.
- [HCUP (U.S. Hospitalization Data)](https://hcup-us.ahrq.gov/) - Nationwide inpatient & emergency data for healthcare utilization and cost analysis.

## 🩻 Medical Imaging & Radiology

### Chest & Lungs
- [NIH Chest X-ray (ChestXray-NIHCC)](https://nihcc.app.box.com/v/ChestXray-NIHCC) - ~112,000 chest X-ray images for pneumonia detection and multi-label disease classification.
- [CheXpert (Stanford)](https://stanfordmlgroup.github.io/competitions/chexpert/) - ~224,000 chest X-rays for weak supervision and uncertainty-aware classification.
- [CheXpert Plus](https://aimi.stanford.edu/shared-datasets) - 223,462 unique pairs of radiology reports and chest X-rays.
- [LIDC-IDRI](https://wiki.cancerimagingarchive.net/pages/viewpage.action?pageId=70225643) - Lung CT scans focusing on pulmonary nodules.
- [COVID-19 Radiography Database](https://www.kaggle.com/tawsifurrahman/covid19-radiography-database) - Chest X-ray images for COVID-19 and pneumonia detection.

### Brain & Neurology
- [OASIS Brain MRI](https://www.oasis-brains.org/) - Brain MRI for healthy aging & Alzheimer’s disease analysis.
- [OpenNeuro](https://openneuro.org/) - Free, open platform for sharing and accessing neuroimaging datasets (fMRI, EEG, MEG).
- [Human Connectome Project (HCP)](https://www.humanconnectome.org/) - High-resolution neuroimaging datasets to map human brain connections.
- [ADNI (Alzheimer’s Disease Neuroimaging Initiative)](http://adni.loni.usc.edu/) - Longitudinal multisite study for early detection of Alzheimer's.

### Oncology & Dermatology
- [TCIA (The Cancer Imaging Archive)](https://www.cancerimagingarchive.net/) - CT, MRI, PET images covering multiple cancer types.
- [HAM10000](https://dataverse.harvard.edu/dataset.xhtml?persistentId=doi:10.7910/DVN/DBW86T) - ~10,000 dermoscopy images for melanoma detection and skin lesion classification.
- [DDI (Diverse Dermatology Images)](https://aimi.stanford.edu/shared-datasets) - Deeply curated, pathologically confirmed image dataset with diverse skin tones.

### Other Imaging
- [EchoNet-Dynamic](https://aimi.stanford.edu/shared-datasets) - 10,030 labeled echocardiogram videos and human expert annotations.
- [MRNet (Knee MRIs)](https://aimi.stanford.edu/shared-datasets) - 1,370 knee MRI exams performed at Stanford.
- [MURA (MSK X-rays)](https://aimi.stanford.edu/shared-datasets) - Musculoskeletal radiographs containing 40,561 images from 14,863 studies.

## 🧬 Genomics & Bioinformatics

- [The Cancer Genome Atlas (TCGA)](https://portal.gdc.cancer.gov/) - Genomic, epigenomic, transcriptomic, and proteomic data across 33 cancer types.
- [UK Biobank](https://www.ukbiobank.ac.uk/) - In-depth genetic and health information from ~500,000 volunteer participants.
- [1000 Genomes Project](https://www.internationalgenome.org/data-portal/data-collection/30x-grch38) - Whole-genome sequencing for population genetics and variant analysis.
- [gnomAD](https://gnomad.broadinstitute.org/) - Large-scale variant database with 140,000+ exomes & genomes.

## 📈 Physiological Signals & Wearables

- [PhysioNet](https://physionet.org/) - Renowned repository of physiological signals and open medical data.
- [PTB-XL ECG Dataset](https://physionet.org/content/ptb-xl/1.0.0/) - Large-scale 12-lead ECG dataset with 21,000+ recordings for arrhythmia detection.
- [MIT-BIH Arrhythmia Database](https://physionet.org/content/mitdb/1.0.0/) - Standard test material for evaluation of arrhythmia detectors.
- [NSRR Sleep Datasets](https://sleepdata.org/) - Polysomnography & sleep signals for sleep disorder detection.

## 📝 Medical NLP & Text

- [CORD-19](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/allen-institute-for-ai/CORD-19-research-challenge) - Scientific literature corpus with 1M+ COVID-19 research papers.
- [BioASQ](http://bioasq.org/) - Biomedical question answering and semantic indexing dataset.
- [Medical Transcriptions](https://www.kaggle.com/tboyle10/medicaltranscriptions) - Medical transcription data for NLP tasks.

## 🇧🇷 Brazilian Public Health Data

- [DATASUS / TABNET](https://datasus.saude.gov.br/informacoes-de-saude-tabnet/) - Official health information system of the Brazilian Ministry of Health.
- [Portal de Dados Abertos do SUS](https://dadosabertos.saude.gov.br/) - Open data portal for the Brazilian Unified Health System (SUS).

## 📊 Epidemiology & General Health

- [Pima Indians Diabetes Dataset](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/uciml/pima-indians-diabetes-database) - Tabular clinical data for diabetes risk prediction.
- [Global Health Observatory (WHO)](https://www.who.int/data/gho) - WHO's gateway to health-related statistics for its 194 Member States.
- [Global Health Data Exchange (GHDx)](https://ghdx.healthdata.org/global-health-data-exchange) - Comprehensive catalog of surveys, censuses, vital statistics, and other health-related data.
